Crosswalk K-12

Crosswalk K-12 transforms school transportation by providing real-time visibility and control, from precise 5-second vehicle tracking to automated student ridership monitoring and intelligent route optimization. Our comprehensive platform empowers district leaders with the necessary tools to improve safety, increase efficiency, deliver peace of mind to parents, and reduce operational costs.

Town Transit

Town Transit streamlines public transportation for small and medium-sized agencies by delivering enterprise-grade capabilities - from real-time tracking and automated stop announcements, to comprehensive ridership analytics and route optimization. PatrolGPS

PatrolGPS is a GPS tracking system for law enforcement that provides real-time location data, speed reports, and updates on lights and sirens. It helps 911 operators and police leaders manage resources and effectively coordinate deployments. The system also monitors officer behavior to enhance safety and response times.
