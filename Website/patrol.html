PATROLGPS

PatrolGPS is a GPS tracking system for law enforcement that provides real-time location data, speed reports, and updates on lights and sirens. It helps 911 operators and police leaders manage resources and coordinate deployments more effectively. The system also monitors officer behavior, which enhances safety and response times.
