Comprehensive Dispatch Solution

Our dispatch integration system provides municipal fleet managers with powerful tools to monitor vehicles in real-time, communicate effectively with drivers, and coordinate operations efficiently across departments.

Complete Fleet Visibility

Maintain total awareness of your municipal fleet with our comprehensive monitoring system:

  • Real-time location tracking for all vehicles on a unified map interface
  • Live status updates including vehicle condition and availability
  • Customizable map views by department, vehicle type, or assignment
  • Historical route playback for analysis and review

Seamless Communication

Stay connected with your entire fleet through integrated messaging tools:

  • Direct messaging to individual drivers for specific instructions
  • Group communication channels for coordinated operations
  • Pre-set message templates for common instructions
  • Acknowledgment tracking to confirm receipt of critical communications

How It Works

Our dispatch integration system works seamlessly with your municipal operations:

1Centralized Dashboard
