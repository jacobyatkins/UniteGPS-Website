EVENT-BASED TRIGGERS

Monitor critical vehicle systems and equipment with our intelligent event-based trigger system. Receive instant notifications, set customizable monitoring parameters, and enhance emergency response capabilities throughout your municipal operations.

Intelligent Event Monitoring System

Our customizable event-based trigger system gives fleet managers the tools they need to monitor critical vehicle systems, track specialized equipment, and improve emergency response times by providing immediate notification when important events occur.

Customized for Your Fleet

Every municipal fleet has different equipment and operational needs. Our system allows you to:

  • Monitor critical vehicle systems (lights, sirens, specialized equipment)
  • Create department-specific monitoring protocols
  • Customize notification recipients for different trigger events
  • Track specialized vehicles (snow plows, dump trucks, elevated lifts)

Real-Time Response Capability

When a monitored event occurs, our system springs into action:

  • Instant email notifications sent to designated personnel
  • Detailed event information including location, equipment status, and vehicle details
  • Map link for immediate visual reference of the event location
  • Automatic logging for comprehensive reporting and operational analysis

How It Works

Our event-based trigger system works seamlessly with your fleet management operations:

1Configure Your Triggers

Set up customized event triggers for different vehicle systems, equipment types, and operational needs through our intuitive admin interface.

2Real-time Monitoring

Our system continuously monitors vehicle systems and equipment status, identifying when critical events occur across your fleet.

3Instant Notification

When a monitored event is detected, notifications are automatically sent to designated recipients for immediate response and coordination.

4Documentation

All events are logged with detailed information including location, time, equipment status, and vehicle details for comprehensive record-keeping.

5Analysis & Reporting

Generate detailed reports to identify patterns, evaluate response times, and optimize equipment utilization across your municipal operations.

6Operational Improvement

Use the data to implement targeted training programs, refine response protocols, and enhance overall operational efficiency.

Configuration Options

Our flexible system lets you customize event triggers to fit your specific municipal needs:

Trigger Types

  • Emergency equipment activation (lights, sirens, PA systems)
  • Specialized vehicle equipment (plows, salt spreaders, lift arms)
  • Vehicle status changes (doors open/closed, engine on/off)
  • Custom triggers for department-specific equipment

Notification Options

  • Instant email alerts to single or multiple recipients
  • Escalation protocols for prolonged activations
  • Department-specific notification routing
  • Customizable alert frequency and threshold settings

Reporting Features
