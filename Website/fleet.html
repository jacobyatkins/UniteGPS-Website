UNITEGPS FLEET

Our Fleet solution enables precise municipal fleet tracking and departmental reporting for city governments, giving them complete visibility and control over their vehicles. This robust solution helps cities optimize daily operations, improve efficiency, and make data-driven decisions to allocate resources better and serve their communities.

Driver Dash Cam Monitoring

  • Real-time updates with filters by vehicle, driver, event type, and severity
  • Dynamic workflow to heighten driver performance and safety culture

Driver Behavior Insights

  • Comprehensive monitoring of vehicle idling, speeding, tampering, and performance metrics to reduce operational costs and identify areas for improvement.
  • Data-driven insights to enhance driver accountability, safety, and operational efficiency.

Event-Based Triggers

  • Instant notifications when critical vehicle systems activate (lights, sirens, specialized equipment) for improved emergency response and efficiency.
