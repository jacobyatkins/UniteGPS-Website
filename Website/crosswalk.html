CROSSWALK K-12

Crosswalk K-12 transforms school transportation by providing real-time visibility and control - from precise 5-second vehicle tracking to automated student ridership monitoring and intelligent route optimization. Our comprehensive platform empowers district leaders with the necessary tools to improve safety, increase efficiency, deliver peace of mind to parents, and reduce operational costs.
