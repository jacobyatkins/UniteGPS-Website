OUR STORY

Our Story

Journey Beyond Maps

In an era of growth-at-all-costs startups, UniteGPS has chosen a different path. No aggressive marketing campaigns. No investor roadshows. Just relentless focus on solving real-life problems and transforming how the nation moves—one bus route at a time.

Since 2014, UniteGPS has operated with a central concept: actually listening to the people who move our communities. No venture capital pitches. No Silicon Valley buzzwords. Just comprehensive solutions built for the real heroes of transportation—the drivers, dispatchers, and administrators keeping the country connected.

Invisible Infrastructure

Behind every school morning, every transit route, and every emergency response lies an intricate choreography of vehicles and personnel. UniteGPS doesn't just map this dance—it orchestrates it.

Their flagship platform, Crosswalk K-12, powers transportation for over 50 school districts nationwide. But their ecosystem extends beyond education with TownTransit, Fleet, and PatrolGPS—forming a comprehensive suite that serves public transportation, municipal governments, and law enforcement agencies.

Human-First Approach

"We see what others miss," explains the UniteGPS philosophy. "The bus driver who knows every child's name. The dispatcher juggling multiple crises. The administrator stretching budgets to breaking points."

This human understanding shapes everything the company builds. When one district needed a streamlined field trip request system, UniteGPS didn't upsell—they built it and shared it with every client. Such a commitment to shared innovation means every customer benefits from the entire network.

Growth comes organically from this approach—transportation directors sharing success stories at regional meetings, superintendents comparing notes, communities witnessing firsthand how smart technology can transform their transportation ecosystems.

Beyond Transportation

What UniteGPS has built transcends simple mapping or tracking. They've created living digital ecosystems that adapt to each community's unique patterns and needs—turning transportation from a logistical challenge into a strategic advantage.

For the schools, transit authorities, municipalities, and police departments they serve, UniteGPS doesn't just provide software—they deliver peace of mind, operational clarity, and the freedom to focus on what matters most: the people being transported.
