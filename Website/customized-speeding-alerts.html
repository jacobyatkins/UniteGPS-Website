CUSTOMIZED SPEEDING ALERTS

Take proactive control of your fleet's safety with our intelligent speeding alert system. Receive instant notifications, set customized thresholds, and promote accountable driving behavior throughout your municipal operations.

Intelligent Speed Monitoring System

Our customizable speeding alert system gives fleet managers the tools they need to protect their drivers, vehicles, and the public by monitoring speeding events and providing immediate notification when violations occur.

Tailored to Your Needs

Every municipality has different safety requirements across various zones and vehicle types. Our system allows you to:

  • Set different speed thresholds by zone type (school, residential, highway)
  • Create department-specific rules and protocols
  • Customize notification recipients for different alert levels
  • Adjust sensitivity levels based on vehicle classification

Immediate Response Capability

When a speeding event occurs, our system springs into action:

  • Instant email notifications sent to designated personnel
  • Detailed alert information including location, speed, and posted limit
  • Map link for immediate visual reference of the incident location
  • Automatic logging for comprehensive reporting and analysis

How It Works

Our speeding alert system works seamlessly with your fleet management operations:

1Configure Your Thresholds

Set up customized speed thresholds for different zones, roads, and vehicle types through our intuitive admin interface.

2Real-time Monitoring

Our system continuously monitors GPS data from your fleet, comparing actual speeds against posted limits and your custom thresholds.

3Instant Notification

When a vehicle exceeds your defined threshold, notifications are automatically sent to designated recipients for immediate action.

4Documentation

All speeding events are logged with detailed information including location, time, speed, driver, and department for comprehensive record-keeping.

5Analysis & Reporting

Generate detailed reports to identify patterns, problem areas, or recurring issues that require attention and training.

6Continuous Improvement

Use the data to implement driver training programs, policy adjustments, and safety initiatives to reduce future violations.
